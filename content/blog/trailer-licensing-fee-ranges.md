What made these placements possible? SyncMaster’s infrastructure.

## What It Paid: Understanding Fee Ranges

The fees generated from these placements highlight the significant earning potential of sync licensing compared to streaming. While exact figures are often confidential, the *ranges* are illustrative:

*   **Premium TV Ad (30-second spot):** Expect $10,000–$75,000+. For a trailer, this can be even higher, given the critical role music plays in driving audience acquisition.
*   **Streaming Original (Netflix Scene):** A single scene can yield $5,000–$20,000. A trailer for a major streaming release could command significantly more.
*   **Global NGO Campaign:** Fees can range from $5,000 to $50,000+, depending on the campaign's reach and duration. The Lagos composer's placement falls into this bracket, demonstrating substantial earning potential for impactful work.
*   **Major Theatrical Film Trailer:** For a prominent trailer, fees can easily range from $20,000 to $150,000+, particularly if the track is featured prominently or custom edits are required.

These figures underscore a critical point: a single sync placement can generate more revenue than years of streaming royalties. For composers, understanding these **trailer licensing fee ranges** is vital for setting expectations and valuing their work appropriately.

## What Other Composers Can Learn

The success of these composers isn't accidental. It's a direct result of having their work align with the demands of the sync market.

1.  **Prioritize Rights Clearance:** If you have uncleared samples or are signed to a label that holds masters, your path to sync is blocked. Focus on original compositions and understand who owns your master and publishing rights.
2.  **Master Your Metadata:** Invest time in ensuring your music is tagged correctly. This includes ISRC codes, BPM, key, and descriptive tags. Use services like TuneCore or DistroKid to manage your ISRC registration.
3.  **Prepare Your Stems:** When a supervisor needs a specific edit, having stems ready is non-negotiable. Organize your project files so you can easily export individual instrument tracks.
4.  **Understand Your Value:** Don't undervalue your work. Research typical sync fees for different media types. A placement in a trailer for a major film or a global ad campaign is a high-value asset.
